    • Good location The hotel is in a good location - not far from Circular Quay, Opera House, Bridge and Darling Harbour. Shops are also close by. Room has everything you need - we paid a special rate due to construction, so we were pleased with what we paid for ($105 for the night). I'm not sure I'd pay the full price though. Rooms were clean with 2 large beds (maybe a bit smaller than a double bed per person).Car parking is a bit of a nightmare and is limited. The sign is small and entrance is round the corner from the hotel - once you've mastered the one way system (tip - if you are coming across Harbour Bridge, stay in a left hand lane).Costs $20 a night.Food and internet looked pricey - there are much cheaper options near by. Reception staff were fine and answered any questions.
